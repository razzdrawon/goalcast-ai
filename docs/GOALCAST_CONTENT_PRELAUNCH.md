## The "Tag Your Bar" Campaign

This is the core mechanic of the pre-launch. Fans recruit venues for you.

**How it works:**
1. GoalCast posts a TikTok/IG asking fans to tag the bar where they're watching the World Cup
2. Fan tags their bar in the comments or in their own post
3. GoalCast DMs every tagged venue: "Your fans want you on GoalCast AI — here's how to join"
4. Venue sees real demand from their own customers → much easier sell than a cold email
5. Fan who tags a venue that joins gets "Founding Fan" early access badge in the app

**The incentive for fans:**
"Tag the bar where you're watching the World Cup 👇 If they join GoalCast as an official venue, you get founding fan access — first on the leaderboard, first to compete for prizes."

**The incentive for venues:**
Instead of a cold pitch, you show up with: "Three of your customers tagged you on our TikTok asking you to join. Here's what they're asking for."
