## Restaurant Pitch (in person)

### What to bring
- Phone with demo open at goalcastai.com/demo
- Business card or note with goalcastai.com

### Opening line
"Hey, I'm Ricardo. I'm launching a World Cup app for fans in Atlanta and I want your restaurant to be one of the official watch party venues. Can I show you something quick?"

### The 3-minute pitch

**1. Show the demo** (goalcastai.com/demo)
"This is what fans see. They predict every match, compete on a leaderboard, and play live trivia during the game. The leaderboard shows which venue they're watching from."

**2. Show the venue benefit**
"Before each match, you get a report like this: '47 fans from GoalCast are planning to watch Spain vs Saudi Arabia here on June 21.' You know how many people are coming before they arrive."

**3. The ask**
"To be listed as an official venue, I just need two things:
- Your name and logo in the app
- To sponsor the end-of-tournament prize — a dinner for two, around $80 value

That's it. No other cost. In exchange, fans in Atlanta see your restaurant as their go-to watch party spot all tournament."

**4. Close**
"The first match is June 15 — Spain vs Cape Verde. I need venues confirmed this week so fans can start selecting you. Are you in?"

## Common Objections & Responses

**"We don't know if we'll have watch parties yet"**
"That's exactly why this helps — when fans start selecting your venue in the app, you'll know weeks in advance how many people to expect. It helps you plan."

**"How many users do you have?"**
"We're launching now with the venues as the first step. The fans sign up when they see official venues listed. You being in is what makes fans join — it's a chicken-and-egg, and you help solve it."

**"We don't want to spend money right now"**
"The only cost is the dinner for two at the end — $80 maximum, and it goes to a customer who's been coming to your restaurant all tournament. It's not a cost, it's a retention tool."

**"Can we think about it?"**
"Of course. I'm confirming venues this week because the first match is June 15. I'll follow up in two days — what's the best way to reach you?"

## Venue Onboarding Checklist

Once a venue says yes:

- [ ] Get their name, address, contact email, phone
- [ ] Get their logo (or just name for now)
- [ ] Confirm prize: dinner for two (~$80) or what they choose to offer
- [ ] Add to venue list in app
- [ ] Send confirmation email with how they'll be listed
- [ ] Give them a QR code / link to share with their customers
