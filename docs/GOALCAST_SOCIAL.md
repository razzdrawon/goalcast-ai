## The Venue Signing — Concept

This is the most important content type before June 15. Every venue that signs becomes a post. The goal: by the time you walk into the fifth venue, you can show them four venues already in. Social proof that costs nothing.

### What the "signing" looks like

Not a contract. A moment.

**Option A — The certificate (physical)**
Print a simple branded card or half-page document:

```
──────────────────────────────────────
       GoalCast AI
  Official Watch Party Venue
       World Cup 2026
──────────────────────────────────────
  [Venue Name]
  [City]

  Founding Venue · Sede Fundadora
──────────────────────────────────────
```

Bring two copies. They keep one. You photograph the moment.

**Option B — The tablet/phone (digital)**
Open the demo on your phone or tablet. Let the owner hold it showing their venue name listed. That's the photo.

**The shot you want:**
- Owner holding the certificate or the phone showing the demo
- Handshake if they're up for it
- Their venue in the background — the bar, the screens, the atmosphere
- Natural, not staged. 15 seconds is enough for a TikTok.

## During Tournament (June 15 — July 19)

### Match day rhythm (post 3x per match)

**Pre-match (morning of):**
AI analysis card — win probability bars, key factors, the GoalCast prediction.
Format: graphic or short video. Simple. Repeatable.

Example caption:
```
🤖 AI says: Spain 78% · Draw 12% · Saudi Arabia 10%

Key factors:
🔥 Spain unbeaten in last 6 group stage matches
🛡️ Saudi Arabia conceded 2+ goals in 4 of last 5 away games

What do YOU predict? 👇
Register → link in bio

#SpainvsSaudiArabia #GoalCastAI #WorldCup2026
```

**During match (halftime or live):**
Leaderboard screenshot or trivia question.
"Who's leading at [Venue Name] right now? 🔥"

**Post-match:**
Result + points recap + winner announcement if applicable.
"Correct score prediction = +5 pts. How many got it? 👇"

### Weekly content
- Leaderboard update: top 3 fans per city, with their venue
- Prize winner announcement: "[Fan] won at [Venue Name] this week 🏆"
- Behind the scenes of the team, the venues, the atmosphere

## TikTok vs Instagram — Same content, different format

| | TikTok | Instagram |
|--|--------|-----------|
| Venue signings | 15-30 sec video, casual, real | Photo + Reel version |
| AI Analysis | Quick talking-head or graphic | Carousel (swipe through factors) |
| Leaderboard | Screen recording + reaction | Story + Feed post |
| Tone | More raw, more energy, more Spanish | Slightly more polished |

Don't overthink the difference early on. Post the same thing on both and see where it gets more traction. Double down there.
